Challah

Challah: A Timeless Artisanal Delight
Our challah is a testament to the art of traditional bread-making, crafted with care and precision in our hilltop bakery. This Jewish braided bread is more than just a loaf – it's a celebration of heritage, flavor, and texture.
Each challah begins with a rich dough, enhanced with locally-sourced eggs, a touch of honey, and a hint of vanilla. The dough is left to rise slowly, developing complex flavors and a tender crumb. Our bakers then skillfully hand-braid each loaf, creating the iconic shape that's as beautiful as it is delicious.
Before baking, we brush each challah with an egg wash, ensuring that signature golden-brown, slightly crisp crust that gives way to a pillowy-soft interior. The result is a bread that's lightly sweet, incredibly tender, and utterly irresistible.
Versatile and delectable, our challah shines in many roles. Serve it fresh with your Friday night dinner, use it for the most indulgent French toast you've ever tasted.

French Apple Tart

Indulge in the exquisite charm of French apple tarts, a perfect harmony of flavors and textures. These elegant pastries start with a base of crisp, flaky mille-feuille, providing a delicate foundation that shatters beautifully with each bite.
Nestled atop this buttery layer is a luscious almond cream filling, its rich nuttiness complementing the other elements perfectly. The crowning glory is a careful arrangement of thinly sliced tart apples, their natural sweetness enhanced by a light dusting of sugar and fragrant cinnamon.
As they bake, the apples soften slightly while maintaining a pleasant bite, creating a delightful contrast to the creamy almond layer and crisp pastry beneath. The result is a sophisticated dessert that balances sweet and tart flavors, crisp and smooth textures, in a truly French culinary tradition.

Hallaca

An hallaca is a beloved Venezuelan Christmas delicacy - a corn dough parcel filled with a rich stew of tender meat, olives, raisins, and capers, all wrapped in fragrant banana leaves. Each handcrafted bundle reveals layers of flavor, from the soft yellow masa to the perfectly seasoned filling studded with colorful bell peppers. Our hallacas are made following time-honored family recipes, bringing the warmth of Venezuelan tradition to your holiday table.

Tequeño (Venezuelan Cheese Stick)

The tequeño - Venezuela's irresistible answer to the perfect appetizer. A crispy, golden-brown pastry wrapped around a core of rich, melty white cheese. Each bite offers a satisfying crunch that gives way to a warm, gooey center. Hand-rolled and freshly baked, these finger-sized delights are perfect for dipping and guaranteed to disappear fast at any gathering. Simple yet addictive - it's the South American twist on feel-good dishes that you won't be able to resist.
