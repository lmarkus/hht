Our ProductsBreads

From centuries-old European recipes to beloved Latin American specialties, our bakery celebrates the world's diverse baking traditions under one roof. Each loaf tells a story of time-honored techniques, carefully selected ingredients, and the skilled hands of our master bakers. Whether you're craving a crusty French baguette, a sweet Venezuelan piñita, or one of our specially developed gluten-free loaves that don't compromise on taste or texture, our breads are crafted daily to bring the warmth and aroma of a traditional bakehouse to your table.

Pumpkin Bread

Fall in love with our pumpkin bread! This cozy loaf is autumn in edible form. We've packed it with real pumpkin, walnuts, and cranberries for that perfect fall flavor. A hint of cinnamon and vanilla makes each slice smell like a dream.
Slice it open to reveal a soft, golden interior that's begging for a swipe of butter. It's great for breakfast, snacking, or impressing guests at dinner.
Made from scratch with wholesome ingredients, it's a treat you can feel good about. The walnuts give it a nice crunch, while the cranberries add a pop of tartness. And don't get us started on that crust - it's scored to look just like a real pumpkin!

These rolls prove that going gluten-free doesn't mean giving up life's simple pleasures.

Cinnamon Rolls

An optional velvety maple glaze crowns each roll, draping over the curves and settling into every crevice with its rich, woodsy sweetness.

Challah

Challah: A Timeless Artisanal Delight
Our challah is a testament to the art of traditional bread-making, crafted with care and precision in our hilltop bakery. This Jewish braided bread is more than just a loaf – it's a celebration of heritage, flavor, and texture.
Each challah begins with a rich dough, enhanced with locally-sourced eggs, a touch of honey, and a hint of vanilla. The dough is left to rise slowly, developing complex flavors and a tender crumb. Our bakers then skillfully hand-braid each loaf, creating the iconic shape that's as beautiful as it is delicious.
Before baking, we brush each challah with an egg wash, ensuring that signature golden-brown, slightly crisp crust that gives way to a pillowy-soft interior. The result is a bread that's lightly sweet, incredibly tender, and utterly irresistible.
Versatile and delectable, our challah shines in many roles. Serve it fresh with your Friday night dinner, use it for the most indulgent French toast you've ever tasted.

Piñitas (Sweet Bread Rolls)

These Venezuelan sweet bread rolls are a tasty treat that's hard to resist. Sold in a tray of nine, they boast golden-brown tops sprinkled with sparkling sugar. Made from a rich, buttery milk bread dough, these rolls are incredibly soft and fluffy.
Each bite reveals a tender, melt-in-your-mouth texture with a perfect balance of sweetness and buttery flavor. The milk in the recipe contributes to their exceptional softness, while the butter adds a rich, creamy taste.
The sugar topping provides a subtle crunch and extra sweetness that complements the pillowy interior. These rolls smell heavenly, filling the air with the aroma of freshly baked, buttery bread.
Ideal for breakfast, snacks, or any time you crave something sweet and comforting, these rolls pair wonderfully with coffee or tea. Their irresistible combination of softness, butteriness, and subtle sweetness makes them a crowd-pleaser for all ages.

Pan De Jamon (Venezuelan Ham Bread)

A Christmas table masterpiece, this Venezuelan Pan de Jamón is a harmonious spiral of soft, pillowy bread enveloping layers of premium ham, sweet raisins, briny olives, and crisp bacon. Each slice reveals a beautiful swirl of festive ingredients, perfectly balanced between sweet and savory. The golden-brown crust gives way to a tender interior, where every bite tells the story of Venezuelan holiday traditions. Serves beautifully warm or at room temperature - an essential part of any Christmas celebration.

Tequeño (Venezuelan Cheese Stick)

The tequeño - Venezuela's irresistible answer to the perfect appetizer. A crispy, golden-brown pastry wrapped around a core of rich, melty white cheese. Each bite offers a satisfying crunch that gives way to a warm, gooey center. Hand-rolled and freshly baked, these finger-sized delights are perfect for dipping and guaranteed to disappear fast at any gathering. Simple yet addictive - it's the South American twist on feel-good dishes that you won't be able to resist.
