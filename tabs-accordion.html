Although the commercial kitchen we use is not a strictly gluten free facility, the Bakery takes the utmost care and goes the extra mile to make sure that our products do not become contaminated. This includes extra sterilization of every tool, work surface and oven we use. The space in which we work is also isolated from the rest of the kitchens. The facility is used by many gluten free food companies and is recognized as a gluten free-friendly facility.

All of our products are absolutely gluten free, but may contain other allergens such as casein and nuts. While we do not directly use soy products, our baked goods may contain soy lecithin through our chocolate. The complete ingredient list for each baked good is available on the website so you can make sure that you are choosing a product that is safe for you to enjoy. If you would like to place a custom order for baked goods that are modified to meet your dietary restrictions let us know!
