The Bakery started in 1986 as a little bakery cafe and pizzeria on Willamette Street in Eugene, Oregon. Popularity and local demand for their products grew, and a larger production facility was needed.

Wholesale operations began in the early 1990s and the bakery began supplying Eugene markets with locally made organic breads. Today our Bakery offers a wide variety of breads, pizza dough, granola, cookies and hummus-style garbanzo spreads.

Natural Ingredients

Our products are hand-made with only the finest ingredients and natural starters. No added preservatives will be found.

Bread Storage

All of our storage facilities are equipped with climate control and are certified for food storage.

Bread Freezing

If you buy loaves to freeze, it's best to do it while the bread is still fresh. For the best result, wrap each loaf in plastic wrap.
